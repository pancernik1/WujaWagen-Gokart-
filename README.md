# Indroduction to the project
## Who are we?
We are three teenagers (3x14yo and 16yo) from Poland.
## How did the project start?
During our winter break we wanted to do something productive and we all
enjoy engineering we ended up deciding to make a cheap gokart
## What are the goals?
1.Make a gokart that can take you from point A to point B. (prefferably without crashing.)
2.Learn something about engineering.
3.Have fun!
# The build
The kart will be powered by a 7.0HP OHV Honda GX160 clone.
We will use a 20mm centrifugal clutch with a 13t gear to transfer the momentum
to a 39t gear mounted on the rear axle via a 76 segment 420 chain.
This combination will give a 3:1 final gear ratio which should result in enough power for
going uphill while also being able to reach speeds above 60km/h (if it doesnt fall apart) on straights.

# Parts
## Drivetrain
- a [rear axle kit](https://allegro.pl/oferta/oska-tyl-atv-buggy-gokart-1000mm-kpl-bri-18114432281) (actually our cheapest and easily accesable option)
- fake [honda gx160](https://allegro.pl/oferta/silnik-spalinowy-zageszczarka-gx160-7-0-km-ohv-walek-20mm-ubijarka-agregat-12961306488?utm_feed=aa34192d-eee2-4419-9a9a-de66b9dfae24&utm_source=google&utm_medium=freelisting) that makes ~7HP
- 13t 20mm [centrifugal clutch](https://pl.aliexpress.com/item/1005007549539747.html) (just pray that it doesn't break and you're fine)
- [420 76 chain](https://pl.aliexpress.com/item/1005006304294128.html)
## Frame
- 30x30x3mm [carbon steel square profiles](https://e-materials.pl/products/profil-stalowy-zamkniety-30x30x3-mm-zimnogiety-s235jr?variant=50170620150102)
- a TIG welder to attach them to each other
## Steering, Brakes & Tires
- [spindles](https://pl.aliexpress.com/item/1005009372667215.html) 78mm
- [steering kit from AliExpress](https://pl.aliexpress.com/item/1005010035577507.html) since it would be hard to implement our own and it's quite cheap
- a disc brake from our friends bike (he agreed to this trust me)
- rims and tires will be the cheapest when sourced locally but some examples are included inside the [BoM.csv](https://github.com/pancernik1/WujaWagen-Gokart-/blob/main/BoM.csv)
